Our Story

Founded with the vision to revolutionize food delivery, foodpanda has grown to become one of the leading platforms connecting customers with their favorite restaurants and grocery stores.

Our Mission

To make food accessible for everyone, everywhere, by connecting customers with the best restaurants and grocery stores in their area.

Global Presence

Operating in multiple countries across Asia, we continue to expand our services to bring delicious food and groceries to more customers.

Innovation

We constantly innovate to improve our platform, making ordering food and groceries easier and more convenient for our customers.

Sustainability

We're committed to sustainable practices, from eco-friendly packaging to supporting local businesses in our communities.
